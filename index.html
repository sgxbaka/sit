🏛️ About The College

Sitamarhi Institute of Technology (SIT) is located in the heart of Sitamarhi district, Bihar. Situated at Village Gosainpur, Post Office Rasulpur, Sitamarhi – 843302, the institute was established with a vision to bring quality technical education to the students of Bihar.


The institute offers the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). With a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, SIT prepares students for successful careers in the technology industry.


The institute has well-equipped computer laboratories, a resourceful library, experienced and dedicated faculty members, and a vibrant campus life that helps students develop both academically and personally.

⚡ Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

The ECE Department focuses on electronics, communication systems, signal processing and embedded systems. Students gain hands-on experience in circuit design, microprocessors and modern communication technologies.

Basic ElectronicsDigital ElectronicsSignals & Systems MicroprocessorsWireless Communication

⚙️ Mechanical Engineering (ME)

The Mechanical Engineering Department covers thermodynamics, manufacturing processes, machine design, fluid mechanics and industrial engineering to prepare students for careers in manufacturing, automotive and energy sectors.

Engineering MechanicsThermodynamicsManufacturing Tech Fluid MechanicsMachine Design

🏗️ Civil Engineering (CE)

Civil Engineering at SIT trains students in structural analysis, construction management, environmental engineering and transportation systems to build a strong foundation for infrastructure development.

Structural AnalysisConcrete TechnologySurveying Environmental EngineeringTransportation Engineering
